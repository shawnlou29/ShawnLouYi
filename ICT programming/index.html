Xiao Tech

The Xiao Tech: "A local friend for a traveler that offers experiential and tailor-made travel programs." We are a versatile and transparent platform where users can find exclusive and immersive experiences, such as living a villager's lifestyle for a few days or studying traditional craftsmanship. Or our users can just complete a tiny questionnaire and get a highly tailored customer made travel schedule in seconds by bookings in a click and a 24/7 live concierge while traveling. Business model: We combine tours, unique events, lodging, transportation, etc., and either sell tours directly in the form of experiences, or provide customized experiences through an AI-based platform. We charge clients a small service fee rather than any commissions.We also plan to charge our suppliers a very small annual membership fee. Technology: We have been manually providing our services, i.e., we have been designing our customized travel plans, making all the bookings for the past two years. (We have currently given them one year free as per our agreement.) Technology: We also have 24/7 customer support, and for any customer, that's a lot of work.Ruby, a former client from New Zealand, is an example. It took her seven days to construct an itinerary, and it took us another four to five days to make her reservations, and we provided 24/7 help. We can reduce this time to about one second via AI (itinerary building and booking). We are finalizing the first step of our product right now. In future phases of our product (we've split it into five phases) With a combination of augmented reality and artificial intelligence, we want to turn it into a utility app that allows a user to understand a place he or she is heading to or is already in, something that helps in terms of audio guides/video guides, enhanced accessibility, regular travel plans, suggestions, tips and tricks and much more.
